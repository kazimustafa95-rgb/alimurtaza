SEIYON+

SEIYON+ is a digital car subscription platform that redefines traditional car leasing into a flexible, mobile first experience. Built in partnership with Seiyon Cars Plus, the platform allows users to browse vehicles, subscribe instantly, and manage their plans without long term commitments. Designed for modern lifestyles, SEIYON+ delivers convenience, transparency, and freedom across both web and mobile.

Problem

Traditional car leasing is rigid, time consuming, and lacks flexibility for modern users.

Solution

Built a connected web and mobile ecosystem that enables instant car subscriptions with flexible plan management and seamless booking.

Outcome

  • • Simplified vehicle access through a fully digital subscription flow
  • • Improved user experience with fast onboarding and plan management
  • • Scalable architecture ready to support growing fleets and users

Key Features

  • Flexible car subscription plans with easy switching
  • Instant vehicle booking and access
  • Unified web and mobile ecosystem
  • Real time subscription and account management

Web App: React Mobile App: React Native Backend: Node.js
