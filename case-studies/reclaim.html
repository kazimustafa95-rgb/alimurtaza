Reclaim

Reclaim is a private recovery platform designed to support individuals overcoming addiction through faith guided structure and behavioral science. Built as a safe judgment free space it helps users rebuild identity discipline and accountability at their own pace.

Get it on Google Play

Problem

Many recovery tools lack privacy structure or emotional alignment leaving users unsupported and disconnected from long term healing.

Solution

Designed a secure digital experience combining faith based encouragement with proven behavioral science tools to guide reflection habit building and personal growth..

Outcome

  • • High early retention
  • • Positive user feedback
  • • Stable performance across devices

Key Features

  • Habit tracking
  • Progress visualization
  • Lightweight user flows
