Heal Like Me

Heal Like Me is a doctor portfolio and appointment booking mobile app designed to simplify how patients discover and connect with healthcare professionals. The platform allows doctors to list their profiles, showcase expertise, and manage availability, while patients can easily search, compare, and book appointments in a few simple steps.

View Prototype

Problem

Patients struggle to find the right doctor and book appointments through fragmented and inefficient systems.

Solution

Built a centralized mobile experience that connects doctors and patients through searchable profiles and streamlined appointment booking.
