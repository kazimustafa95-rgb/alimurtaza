Starting Points

Starting Points is a collaborative innovation platform designed to help teams unlock better thinking through structured, guided questions. Originally a consultancy framework used in live workshops, the product was transformed into a digital experience that enables teams to explore ideas, document insights, and build operational curiosity at scale across organizations.

Get it on Google Play

Problem

Teams struggle to capture insights and maintain meaningful collaboration beyond workshops and meetings.

Solution

Built a digital platform that turns guided questioning into an interactive, collaborative workflow for continuous team alignment and innovation.

Outcome

  • • Scalable digital version of a proven consultancy framework
  • • Improved structure for team discussions and insight capture
  • • Enabled asynchronous collaboration across distributed teams

Key Features

  • Guided prompt library for structured team conversations
  • Interactive question engine to unlock new perspectives
  • Collaborative workspace for co creation and progress tracking
  • Centralized insight documentation for long term visibility

Tech Stack
